##  About FoodEasy

FoodEasy is a **mobile-first grocery delivery platform** that brings fresh, high-quality foodstuffs and groceries to your doorstep at prices **cheaper than local markets**. 

We partner directly with top FMCG brands and local farmers, cutting out middlemen to deliver predictable, affordable food packages tailored to Nigerian families.

---

##  Why FoodEasy Matters

### The Problem
- **56% of urban Nigerians** struggle with consistent access to affordable, quality groceries
- Market prices are unpredictable and often inflated by multiple intermediaries
- Busy professionals waste hours shopping for essentials every week
- Existing platforms lack transparent pricing and customized family packages

### Our Solution
FoodEasy delivers **pre-curated food packages** designed for different household sizes and budgets:
-  **20-30% cheaper** than market prices
-  **Fresh produce** directly from trusted suppliers
-  **Predictable pricing** - no surprise costs
-  **Convenient delivery** to your doorstep

---

##  Who It's For(Target Audience)

### Primary Users
- **Working Professionals (25-44 years)** - Save time on grocery shopping
- **Growing Families** - Affordable bulk packages with everything they need
- **Budget-Conscious Households** - Stretch their naira further without sacrificing quality

### Secondary Users
- **NGOs & Corporates** - Bulk orders for welfare programs and employee benefits
- **Small Businesses** - Consistent supply for restaurants and caterers

##  Key Features

###  Smart Package Options
Choose from expertly curated packages or build your own:
- **Basic Package** - Essential staples for singles/small households (₦15,000)
- **Boss Package** - Premium selection for comfort lovers (₦35,000)
- **Family Package** - Complete grocery solution for families (₦50,000)
- **Marketplace** - Mix and match individual items your way

###  Seamless Experience
- **One-Tap Ordering** - Select package, pay, and relax
- **Real-Time Tracking** - Know exactly when your groceries arrive
- **Smart Dashboard** - View orders, track deliveries, manage referrals
- **Flexible Payment** - Card, bank transfer, or cash on delivery

###  Trust & Transparency
- Clear product visuals and quantities for every package
- Direct partnerships with brands (Dangote, Golden Penny, local farmers)
- Quality guarantee on every delivery
- Responsive customer support

---

##  Technology Stack

**Frontend**: React.js with Tailwind CSS for fast, mobile-optimized UI  
**Backend**: Node.js + Express.js for scalable API services  
**Database**: MongoDB for flexible user and order management  
**Payments**: Paystack integration for secure transactions  
**Hosting**: AWS/Railway for reliable infrastructure
